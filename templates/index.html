The Problem: Kessler Syndrome

Space debris threatens all satellites in Low Earth Orbit. A single collision can trigger a cascade effect, destroying thousands of satellites. Current ground-based tracking is too slow for real-time avoidance.

The Solution: Autonomous Edge-AI

Our system uses reinforcement learning to enable satellites to make autonomous collision avoidance decisions in milliseconds, without waiting for ground control commands.

Key Innovations

  • Real-Time: Sub-millisecond inference on satellite hardware
  • Autonomous: No ground-in-the-loop required
  • Fuel-Efficient: Learns optimal maneuvers
  • Deployable: 44KB model runs on embedded systems
